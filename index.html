About Us

Founded in 2008

FTTechnologies is a trusted provider of cloud-based payment solutions for financial institutions, payment facilitators, and networks worldwide.

We deliver secure, scalable systems that meet the highest international standards, including PCI, Visa, and Mastercard compliance.

Our team combines deep domain expertise with a culture of innovation, client commitment, teamwork, and security—driven by a global vision.

Key Milestones

Founded in 2008

FTTechnologies is a trusted provider of cloud-based payment solutions for financial institutions, payment facilitators, and networks worldwide. We deliver secure, scalable systems that meet the highest international standards, including PCI, Visa, and Mastercard compliance. Our team combines deep domain expertise with a culture of innovation, client commitment, teamwork, and security—driven by a global vision.

2008 - Market Entry & First Solution

First production deployment, partnering with Digicel to launch an innovative IVR-based airtime top-up platform in Honduras.

2015 - Platform Solutions at Scale

Launched platform solutions processing Visa and Mastercard transactions.

2019 - Tap to Phone Innovation

Launched Latin America's first Visa and Mastercard branded Tap to Phone solution.

2020 - Innovation Recognized

Earned recognition as one of the Top 10 most innovative companies in Latin America at Visa Everywhere event.

2022 - Record-Breaking Implementation

Deployed Panama Metro System's first contactless card acceptance solution in record time.

2023 - Global Technology Breakthrough

Became the first global solution provider certified by Visa to process Tap to Phone with PIN in the cloud.

2025 - Network Integration Innovation

Developed reverse API solution enabling alternative payment networks to connect seamlessly to Visa's network.
