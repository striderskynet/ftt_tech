About Us

Founded in 2008, FTTechnologies is a trusted provider of cloud-base electronic payment solutions for financial institutions, payment facilitators, and networks worldwide. With over 15 years of industry expertise, we deliver secure, agile, and scalable systems that meet the highest international standards, including PCI, Visa, and Mastercard compliance.

Driven by a global vision, our team blends deep domain knowledge with a culture of innovation, collaboration, and adaptability. We design flexible solutions that evolve with your business, empowering you to navigate the dynamic digital payments landscape with confidence.

At FTTechnologies, we champion financial inclusion and digital transformation. Our commitment to continuous innovation helps clients grow, enhance their brand value, and expand into global markets, securely and seamlessly.

Key Milestones

2008 - Market Entry & First Solution

FTTechnologies entered the market with our first production solution, partnering with Digicel to launch an innovative IVR-based airtime top-up platform in Honduras. This groundbreaking service enabled users to recharge their mobile phones online using credit or debit cards, establishing our foundation in digital payment solutions.

2015 - Platform Solutions at Scale

After two years of dedicated development, FTTechnologies successfully migrated VisaNet Guatemala's (now NeoNet) entire transaction processing to our UNITY platform. The platform launch also enabled Mastercard processing across 135,000+ terminals on day one. Today, NeoNet processes over 1.5 million daily transactions, a 328% increase from the original 350,000 daily transactions.

2019 - Tap to Phone Innovation

FTTechnologies launched the first Visa-branded Tap to Phone solution in Latin America, supported by Visa International and Banco Popular de Costa Rica. Within nine months, we integrated Mastercard capabilities. Our cloud-based Tap to Phone service now serves 25+ clients across 13 Latin American countries.

2020 - Regional Recognition

Selected by Visa to participate in the prestigious Visa Everywhere Initiative event, FTTechnologies competed alongside 50+ FinTech. We presented our Tap to Phone solution with fully digital onboarding and earned recognition as one of the Top 10 most innovative companies in Latin America, the only Central American company to achieve this distinction among competitors from major markets including Mexico and Brazil.

2022 - Record-Breaking Implementation

In partnership with Credicorp Bank of Panama, Sonda, Visa, and Mastercard, FTTechnologies deployed contactless card acceptance for the Panama Metro system in just three months, a record implementation time. All transactions are processed through our cloud-based Transit (MTT) solution, demonstrating our platform's speed and reliability.

2022 - Global Technology Breakthrough

FTTechnologies certified PIN on COTS (SPoC) service for our Cloud-based Tap to Phone Kernel. FTTechnologies card present solutions certified by Visa Acceptance Services (CyberSource), becoming the first global solution to process Tap to Phone with PIN in the cloud.
