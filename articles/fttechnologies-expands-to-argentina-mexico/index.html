FTTechnologies Expands to Argentina & Mexico, Unveiling a Major Rebrand

San Jose, Costa Rica

November 5, 2025

FTTechnologies, a leading provider of cloud-based payment solutions, today announced a comprehensive new brand identity and its expansion into Argentina and Mexico. The rebrand includes a refreshed logo, updated corporate visuals, and messaging that reflects the company's evolution and singular focus on shaping the future of global payments.

Our new brand identity is more than just a new logo; it's a reflection of our journey and our vision for the future. By unifying our brands and expanding our global footprint into key markets like Argentina and Mexico, we are reinforcing our commitment to providing innovative and secure payment solutions for our clients and partners. This is a pivotal moment for FTTechnologies as we accelerate our mission to "Innovating the Way We Pay."

The new operations in Argentina and Mexico mark a significant step in the company's global expansion strategy, bringing FTTechnologies closer to its clients and partners. This expansion allows us to better serve our partners and demonstrates our commitment to delivering cutting-edge payment infrastructure where it's needed most.

About FTTechnologies Founded in 2008, FTTechnologies is a trusted provider of cloud-based payment solutions for financial institutions, payment facilitators that meet the highest international standards, including PCI, Visa, and Mastercard compliance.
